Our Studio

The Studio had it's first showcase in 1999 under the leadership of Herold James, and has had subsequently sucessfull showcases ever since.

We hold one of the worlds largest collection of vintage automobiles. Our studio holds cars from all over the world, some of our collections include, 1967 chevy Corvette, Jaguar E-Type, 1966 Lamborghini Minura, the 1974 redesigned Porsche 911, 1949 Rolls-Royce Dawn Drophead 1962 Ferrari 250 GTO, Aston Martin DB4, and many more.
